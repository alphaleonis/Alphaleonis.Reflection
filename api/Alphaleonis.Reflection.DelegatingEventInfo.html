    Remarks

    Derive from this type and override only those members you have to provide customization in.

    DelegatingEventInfo derives from EventInfo and implements most of the properties and methods of EventInfo. For each member it implements, DelegatingEventInfo automatically delegates to the corresponding member of the internal EventInfo object which was supplied as the argument to the constructor. This internal EventInfo is exposed to deriving classes by the langword_csharp_protected Alphaleonis.Reflection.DelegatingEventInfo.m_eventImpl field.
