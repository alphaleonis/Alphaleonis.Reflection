    Remarks

    Derive from this type and override only those members you have to provide customization in.

    DelegatingMethodInfo derives from MethodInfo and implements most of the properties and methods of MethodInfo. For each member it implements, DelegatingMethodInfo automatically delegates to the corresponding member of the internal MethodInfo object which was supplied as the argument to the constructor. This internal MethodInfo is exposed to deriving classes by the langword_csharp_protected Alphaleonis.Reflection.DelegatingMethodInfo.m_methodImpl field.
